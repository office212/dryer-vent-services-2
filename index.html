Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my dryer vent?

Most homes need a full dryer vent cleaning once a year. If you do a lot of laundry (large family, many loads per week), every 6–9 months is recommended.

How long does a typical cleaning take?

A standard cleaning usually takes 30–45 minutes. Longer or higher vents (2nd floor, roof exits) can take more time.

Will my home get dusty during the cleaning?

No. We use a sealed vacuum and professional brushes designed to keep dust and lint contained while we clean.

How much does dryer vent cleaning cost?

Pricing depends on where the vent exits: Basement/1st floor: $150, 2nd floor: $200, 3rd floor or roof exit: $250. Repairs and new vent installations are priced individually after we see the setup.

Do you repair dryers too?

We focus only on the vent system – ducts, elbows, hoods and airflow. We do not repair or service the dryer appliance itself.
