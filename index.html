Why Dryer Vent Cleaning Matters

A clogged dryer vent makes your dryer work harder, wastes energy and can become a serious fire hazard.

  • Reduced fire risk from lint buildup
  • Shorter drying times and better performance
  • Less wear on your dryer and lower energy bills

Signs You Need a Cleaning

  • Clothes take more than one cycle to dry
  • Dryer or laundry room feels very hot
  • Burning smell when the dryer runs
  • It's been over a year since your last cleaning
