Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers to the most commonly asked questions, check out more frequently asked questions and their responses under the FAQ tab.

How often should I clean my dryer vent?

It is recommended to clean your dryer vent once a year. If you have a large household and do laundry frequently it may be necessary for you to clean it every 6-9 months.

How long does a typical cleaning take?

A standard cleaning usually takes 30-45 minutes. Longer runs, roof exits or damaged vents can take more time.

Will my home get dusty during the cleaning?

No. We use a sealed vacuum system and professional brushes that keep dust contained. The work area is left neat and clean.

How much would it cost to clean my dryer vent?

Our pricing structure is based on the location of the dryer within the home. We do not impose additional charges for long vents, bird nests, or heavily clogged vents. Rates are determined solely by the floor on which the dryer is located.

Do you repair dryers too?

We clean, repair, and install dryer vents only. We focus on airflow and duct safety. We do not repair dryers themselves, but we work with local appliance repair companies when needed.
