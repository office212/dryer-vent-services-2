FAQ

How often should I clean my dryer vent?

At least once a year for typical households. High usage, pets, or longer vent runs may need every 6–9 months.

How long does the service take?

Most cleanings take 45–90 minutes depending on vent length and severity of buildup.

Do you service apartments and condos?

Yes — we regularly service multi-family buildings across NYC and NJ.
